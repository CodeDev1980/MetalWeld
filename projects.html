Arizona

Metal-Weld was incorporated in November 1979. The corporate headquarters has been located in Arizona since inception. We have recently completed the structural steel erection of the Scottsdale Quarter project, Building B and C.
A few of our other accomplishments in Arizona are:

  • Christ's Church of the Valley
  • Kierland Commons Phase 5A
  • NAU Conference Center
  • GCC Life Science Building

Nevada

Metal-Weld works daily with some top contractors in the United States. We are currently working on The Hard Rock Hotel and Casino expansion. We recently completed subcontracted fabrication, on a combined total of 10,000 tons on the City Center and new VA Medical Center in Las Vegas. Additionally, we have completed approximately 2600 tons of subcontracted fabrication on the Fountainbleau project.
Other notable projects that we have completed in Nevada are:

  • MGM lion statue (out of bronze)
  • Harley Davidson Cafe
  • Tao Restaurant / Night Club at the Venetian
  • UNLV School of Architecture
  • Green Valley Ranch
  • Arroyo Office Park

Hawaii

We maintain a strong contractor base on all the Hawaiian Islands. We have several notable projects that we have completed in Hawaii:
  • Westin Kaanapali Ocean Resort
  • USS Arizona Memorial
  • Large custom residences - Islands of Maui and Hawaii
  • Maui Sheraton
  • Magic of Polynesia Theater
  • Wal-Mart Maui
  • Cycle City of Honolulu
  • Tony Honda Autoplex

California

We are currently working on the Pala Casino Spa and Resort project.
