Why Choose Us?

Metal-Weld Specialties, Inc. is located in Peoria Arizona and has been family owned and operated since 1962. We adhere to the highest quality standards in the industry. Our team of professionals deliver high quality, complex steel projects to owners, general contractors and developers in Arizona, California, Hawaii and Nevada.

Frequently asked questions

Didn't find your answer in the FAQ? Contact our sales team.

How long will the job take?

Every job has different time lengths and hard to say right off the bat without seeing your project for ourselves. Typically, we include our projected time length in a quote, but we work hard to get it done in a timely manner without sacrificing quality of work.

Will there be hidden charges?

What we charge is usually quoted upfront and that's what you will see for the work to be done. We will work with you on financing options, so that the project can get done and paying for it will be easier on you.

How far do you travel for projects?

At the moment we are currently in Arizona, Nevada, California and Hawaii. But, for traveling expenses and the cost that we quote you, we could travel to your location.
