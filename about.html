About Us

**** Metal-Weld performs most of its fieldwork with our sister company MW Erectors, Inc. We specialize in large steel structures; however, we are more than experienced in all facets of the structural steel industry. ***

Metal-Weld Specialties, Inc.
has been servicing the structural steel industry for over 45 years. We maintain an exceptional team of experienced and dedicated employees. Metal-Weld is an AISC certified fabricator and also is an ISO 9001 certified company. In addition, we are an approved fabricator for the City of Phoenix, City of Los Angeles, and Clark County, Nevada. Our headquarters and fabrication plant are located in Peoria, Arizona allowing us to service our customers in Arizona, Nevada, California, and Hawaii.
